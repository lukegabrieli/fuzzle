About Fuzzle

Fuzzle was created by three tennis fans who wanted a means to actively engage with the Seattle tennis community.

When brainstorming, we knew we wanted two key features:

  1. 1. View other tennis enthusiasts and see their location and skill level
  2. 2. See a map of all public tennis courts in the greater Seattle area

Thus, Fuzzle was born.
