About Fuzzle

Fuzzle was created by three tennis fans who were tired of not being albe to actively engage with the Seattle tennis community. We asked ourselves, "Why isn't there a site where you can go see all of the tennis courts in Seattle and where you can ask other tennis players to meet up?" Thus, Fuzzle was born.
